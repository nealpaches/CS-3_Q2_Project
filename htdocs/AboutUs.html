GET TO KNOW US!

Neal

Neal David E. Paches, the dynamic CEO of NES Daily Corporation, embodies a dedicated commitment to delivering exceptional service and fostering a visually compelling news experience. Exhibiting steadfast confidence in his ability to deliver the latest updates and trending news globally, Neal takes on a pivotal role in ensuring the accuracy and capture of the content presented to our audience. His visionary leadership serves as the driving force behind the corporation's success in capturing viewer attention, transforming the news into not merely an informative source but an engaging spectacle. Neal's forward-thinking vision extends beyond conventional norms, aiming to redefine the consumption and experience of news, thereby solidifying NES Daily as a dependable and aesthetically pleasing platform for daily updates.

Elijah

Elijah Kendrick T. Yap plays an important role as our dedicated photojournalist, excelling at capturing the visual essence of news stories. He not only curates the appealing pictures that complement our news items but also serves as a consistent source of substantiation through his expertly crafted images. Elijah's dedication to accuracy ensures that our audience has a complete and visually immersive grasp of the events we cover. His commitment to delivering powerful photos not only enhances the storytelling experience but also maintains the authenticity and trustworthiness of our news coverage.

Sam

Danielle Samantha M. Amaro works as an information gatherer for NES Daily Corporation. As the news outlet's dedicated researcher, she diligently collects and analyzes data, ensuring the delivery of accurate and insightful material to our discerning audience. Sam is the only female member of the NES Daily Corporation, and she brings a valuable and unique viewpoint to our dynamic newsroom. Apart from her work in data collection, Sam serves as a link between raw information and our readers, expertly translating complex data into easily digestible news items. In her role, she plays a critical role in sustaining the highest standards of journalistic ethics by consistently providing the public with well-researched and informative information.
